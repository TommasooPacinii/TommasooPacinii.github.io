01 — Overview

What is ETS?

ETS is a concept platform designed to simplify energy cost-tracking and sustainability reporting for mid-size companies. The goal was to explore how structural cost data could be presented in a way that reduces cognitive load and improves comprehension for non-technical stakeholders.

02 — The challenge

Energy data is complex. Dashboards make it worse.

Most energy management tools are built for engineers — dense tables, raw numbers, no narrative context. Business teams struggle to extract decisions from the data. The challenge was to design a system that translates complexity into clarity without losing accuracy.

03 — Process

From research to high-fidelity prototype

Starting from competitor analysis and user interviews, I mapped pain points around data interpretation and reporting workflows, then iterated through multiple rounds of wireframes to a tested prototype.

1
Discovery & research
Competitor analysis across 5 energy platforms. Identified key usability gaps in data hierarchy and reporting flows.
2
Information architecture
Mapped user journeys and defined the core navigation structure across 4 main modules: Dashboard, Cost tracking, Reports, Alerts.
3
Wireframing & iteration
3 rounds of low-to-mid fidelity wireframes, refining layout logic and component hierarchy based on internal feedback.
4
High-fidelity design & system
Built a 40-component design system in Figma. Delivered 20+ high-fidelity screens across desktop and mobile breakpoints.
